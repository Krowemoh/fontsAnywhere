Fonts Anywhere

Installation
Drag and drop the button above onto your bookmarks toolbar.

Quick How-to
Search, random or scroll through the fonts and once you've chosen one, copy the <link> tag into your own web page and copy the font-family into your css.

What is Fonts Anywhere?
Fonts Anywhere is a bookmarklet to check out all the fonts in Google Fonts. Instead of dealing with html, css and refreshing, you can sit back, relax and spam the up and down arrow keys while fonts change all around you.

In a rush and just need any font?
Hit that weird icon next to the search bar to see a random font.

Already have a font in mind?
Search for it through that weird area next to the random icon.
